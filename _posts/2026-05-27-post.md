title: Donald Trump is crushing America’s farmers
---

American farmers face mounting financial pressure from Trump's **tariffs**, which increased machinery costs and triggered Chinese retaliation that eliminated soyabean exports, while the Iran conflict has raised diesel and fertiliser prices by **40%**.

Farm bankruptcies rose **46%** in 2025 as input costs soared—land up 6%, seeds 18%, labour **50%**, while crop prices remain stagnant, forcing many operations toward insolvency despite federal subsidy discussions.

Rural communities suffer broader economic damage as struggling farmers **reduce spending**, affecting local businesses from restaurants to equipment dealers, though Trump maintains strong approval ratings among rural voters.
